2018 Strains

LIfter

Oregon CBD

Still looking for that Special Sauce and can't find it? "Lifter" is descendant of Oregon CDB's flagship "Special Sauce" and is known for high yields and a sweet funky taste. Our lifter tested with 18.1% CBDa and a Total CBD of 17.2% with very large and dense flowers. 

Suver Haze

Oregon CBD

"Suver Haze", with a Total CBD of 17.2%, is another descendant from Oregon CBD's "Special Sauce" crossed with "Neville's Haze" in 2015. Suver Haze is a particularly special strain to Oregon CBD because it is the resulting strain of the only Type III Hemp plant found in the F2 test grow from 2015. This strain has a unique and intoxicating terpene profile that must be experienced to be truly appreciated.

Hawaiian Haze

Oregon CBD

This strain of "Hawaiian Haze" was created by Oregon CBD by crossing "ACDC" with "Neville's Haze" to create the highest yield CBD oil plant possible with a terpene profile like those of imported Hawaiian flowers (rich with betacarypholene and humulene). The result is a remarkable tropical hybrid that can be grown in the continental U.S.

Elektra

Oregon CBD

"Elektra" boasts one of the highest CBDa levels available on the market right now. The classic "ACDC" Hybrid crossed with Oregon CBD's pollen donor "ERB" makes this an exceptional and unique strain. Elektra smells and tastes a bit fruity with a light citrus aroma. averaging a Total CBD of 17.5%, Look forward to feeling clear headed and relaxed with this high-CBD relative of the "Cannatonic" strain.
