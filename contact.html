Frequently Asked Questions

Check this list of FAQ's before contacting us, we might already have an answer to your question:

How do I remove an item from my cart?
You cannot remove items from the cart after you have started checkout. Click on the cart icon to return to the main cart page to remove items. You should see an "X" to the right of each item in your cart.

Can you overnight my order?
We do not operate near a major shipping facility and cannot guaruntee overnight shipping. Please expect a 2 day processing time. Our total shipping time is 4-7  business days, but it can sometimes be faster. 

How do I apply a discount code?
Click on "Redeem your coupon" on the first checkout page. Click the cart button at anytime to go back to this page.

Do you have wholesale options?
Yes, we have a wholesale site with untrimmed, trimmed and pre-packaged options. Have a look around and contact us if you are looking for something we don't have listed.
